REQUEST: /artists/<artist_name>
GET parameters
    artist_name — required

RESPONSE: a JSON list of dictionaries of artists whose name contains artist_name.
          Each dictionary in this list will have the following fields.
  artist_name -- (TEXT) the name of the artist
  song_name -- (TEXT) the name of the song
  release_date -- (DATE) the date on which the song was released
  url -- (TEXT) url to the song on Spotify.com

EXAMPLE(S):
  /artists/nat
[{“url”: “https://open.spotify.com/track/1VuAamHM7P3VQQEXYGG3Uu”, “song_name”: “Joy To The World”, “artist_name”: “Nat King Cole”, “release_date_year”: 1960, “release_date_month”: 10, “release_date_day”: 15}, {“url”: “https://open.spotify.com/track/4PS1e8f2LvuTFgUs1Cn3ON”, “song_name”: “The Christmas Song (Merry Christmas To You)“, “artist_name”: “Nat King Cole”, “release_date_year”: 1962, “release_date_month”: 10, “release_date_day”: 15}, {“url”: “https://open.spotify.com/track/0ecC8p17cDNlxHXkuYqeR6”, “song_name”: “Surrender”, “artist_name”: “Natalie Taylor”, “release_date_year”: 2019, “release_date_month”: 10, “release_date_day”: 29}]
